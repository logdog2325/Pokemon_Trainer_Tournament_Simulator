🏆 Mega Evolution Tier List

Pokémon Champions · Reg M-B
Methodology — DOMINANCE / RESULTS-led (win rate still counts): the score is led by how much a Mega actually achieves — event wins + top-cut volume + usage — with win rate kept as a strong secondary factor. Wins and top-cuts are size-weighted (a 200-player major counts far more than an 8-player side event), so the most successful Mega rises to #1 (Floette: 892 teams, 55% win rate, most event wins 🏆). Usage is a stepped bonus (100+ = big, 50+ = solid, under 10 = heavy penalty), so fringe one-offs crater (Absol, 1 team → F). Win rate still pulls the efficient mons up (Scovillain/Blaziken 56% → S) and sinks losers (Sceptile 36% → C). Tiers are cut by 1-D k-means (natural breaks). 🏆 in the corner = event wins. Big number = raw win rate; line below = teams · top-cut. ≥52% · 46–52% · ≤46%.
