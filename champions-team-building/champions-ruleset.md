# Pokémon Champions — Fixed Rule Set (Reg M-B)

Every team must satisfy all of these. Run as a checklist in Phase 7.

## Team rules
- [ ] **Species Clause** — no duplicate species.
- [ ] **Item Clause** — every Pokémon holds a *different* item. (Unlike traditional VGC.)
      A full team needs 6 distinct items.
- [ ] **Final evolutions only** — no NFE Pokémon.
- [ ] All Pokémon auto-leveled to **50**.
- [ ] Bring 4 of 6 to each battle (standard VGC doubles, Team Preview).

## Mega Evolution
- [ ] A team may carry **multiple Mega Stones**, but only **ONE Mega Evolution per battle**.
- [ ] You choose which to evolve at/after Team Preview; the un-evolved stone is **inert** that game.
- [ ] Corollary: put stones on Pokémon whose Mega you'll *actually pick*. A dedicated role-player
      you bring every game is often better with an always-on item than a sometimes-dead stone.

## Stat training ("Stat Points" — replaces EVs/IVs)
- [ ] **66 total stat points** per Pokémon.
- [ ] **Max 32 points** in any single stat.
- [ ] **No IVs** exist. Minimum-speed for Trick Room comes from a Speed-lowering **nature + 0 Speed points**.
- [ ] **Natures still apply** (e.g. Modest, Quiet, Brave, Bold).
- Typical spreads: attacker = 32 offense / 32 HP / 2 filler; wall = 32 HP / split Def+SpD.

## Quick math notes
- 32 points ≈ "max" a stat (analogous to 252 EVs); the 2-point filler ≈ the old "4 EV" leftover.
- Speed control is binary per team: commit to Tailwind **or** Trick Room and build the natures around it.
